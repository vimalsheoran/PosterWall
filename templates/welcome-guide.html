Step 1 : Get An Account

All Poster Wall accounts are created on the request and user information provided by respective societies. So if your society has a Poster Wall account you can login from that account. You can also request accounts (please read the Account Request Policy for more details)

Step 2 : Login

Login with the provided credentials and head to your dashboard. Important notice to all societies please don't share your account details with a lot of memebers.

Step 3 : Create and Publish

Get the control of the dashboard and create and store events on our servers and publish them whenever you want. Please read all the polices related to the usage and terms of use
